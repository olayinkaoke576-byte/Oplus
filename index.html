About Oplus Aluminium

Oplus Aluminium is a professional aluminium fabrication and installation company based in Lagos. We specialize in high-quality aluminium sliding windows, doors, handrails, shower cubicles, and interior aluminium works.

Our commitment to durability, clean finishing, and customer satisfaction sets us apart. With years of experience serving both residential and commercial clients across Lagos, we have built a reputation for excellence in aluminium fabrication.

We work with premium-grade materials and employ modern techniques to deliver solutions that are not only functional but also enhance the aesthetic appeal of your space.
